Hello everyone! I've gotten back into making resources to avoid stress from uni. I mainly focus on tilesets but might give a go to making other stuff later on. I thought I would share them with you guys since I'm not working on any games and I don't want my work to be pointless. Hope you find a use for these, enjoy!

Terms of use:
- These are edits of the RTP, so you need to own a legal copy of RPG Maker MV to use them.
- Credit Kadokawa and me (kmonnie, K, Kai, kmonster, whatever you prefer -but not Clariuski-chan please).
- Please don't repost these with no modification.
- If you edit these, you can repost them but make sure to credit me (and don't do it here please).

Notes:
I usually make my tilesets two different ways.
- Standard alignment: the same as the original RTP. The character's head stops right below the tile.
- Fixed alignment: tiles are shifted up (usually half a tile), so that the character's head covers part of the tiles and it looks like the character can interact with them. It also aligns better with the walls.
DD/MM/YYYY format!
03/07/2019 - Created thread: boxes, sacks and floating islands.
05/07/2019 - Added to thread: kitchen set with a lot of kitchen and hob variations.
19/01/2020 - Added to thread: outside edits set (incomplete) and dining table set (incomplete).

Tilesets - edits:
General edits, with the intention of expanding the original RTP and adding some flavour to your maps.
Outside:
Trees with flowers and grass, different flower pots and baskets, different signs, etc. Just general edits of the outside tiles. Also, I added a face to the scarecrow cause I could. This tileset is incomplete for now, but I'll get back to it, don't worry!
Dining tables:
I thought it'd be nice to have some dining tablets with different food on them. I made a fancy variation for your noble houses and castles. This tileset is also incomplete and I'll get back to it as soon as uni lets me live.

Tilesets - variations:
Made with the intention of adding variation to your maps to make different locations look... well, different.
Wooden boxes:
Storage boxes with different stacking options and contents! Some of these include red apples, grass, wine, blankets and gold bars, as well as the default RTP box contents.
Storage sacks:
Storage sacks with different stacking options and contents! Includes default contents, plus green apples and carrots.
Kitchens
For all your kitchen building needs. Kitchens with different curtain colors (or no curtains at all), cutting boards, sinks, hobs, and even tiny stools and sacks to add in front of them! Add a lot of variation to your houses with this set.
Floating islands:
Some floating islands for your overworld maps, with different locations on top; towers, mountains, camps, temples...

That's all for now! I know it's not much, but I will keep updating this thread as I make new stuff. I'm also quite new to this, so please understand if there's small mistakes. Feel free to point out any issues and I will try to fix them. Thanks a lot!
